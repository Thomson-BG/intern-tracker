Intern Time Clock

This application is for interns to clock in and out of their internship sites and to report absences.

Navigate to the "Clock In/Out" tab, fill in your first name, last name, and employee ID, and then click the "Check In" or "Check Out" button.

Please contact your supervisor immediately to get your time sheet corrected.

Navigate to the "Report Absence" tab, fill in your information and the reason for your absence, and click "Submit Absence."
